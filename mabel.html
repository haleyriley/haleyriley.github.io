Mabel Magazine

Mabel Magazine is a school project.

The mission of the magazine is to create a community and publication that focuses on nonbinary folks and women who play and/or enjoy punk music. My aesthetic approach is grungy and experimental while not sacrificing order and legibility. I wanted the focus of this issue to be on supporting musicians during COVID-19, because the music industry is taking a hard hit, and there are not a lot of apparent resources for musicians.

I found articles from websites and publications that report on similar topics. It was incredibly difficult to find articles about women in the punk scene that are related to COVID-19. However, there are plenty of articles and interviews about male musicians and COVID. This is a good example of how women and non-binary folks are left out of the dialogue and spotlight in punk (and other music scenes.)
